Products

EcoSorb Aerogel is an advanced, eco-friendly material designed to tackle environmental challenges efficiently. Made from agricultural waste, it is engineered for high performance while maintaining a low environmental footprint.

Key Features: EcoSorb Aerogel offers exceptional oil absorption, hydrophobic and oleophilic properties, lightweight design, and eco-friendly, biodegradable materials, making it the perfect solution for sustainable environmental management.
Applications: From oil spill cleanup to produced water treatment and heavy metal removal, EcoSorb Aerogel provides efficient, reusable solutions for industrial waste management, ensuring a cleaner, more sustainable world.
